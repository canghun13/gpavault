How weighted GPA works

A weighted GPA gives extra points for harder courses. AP and IB courses add +1.0, honors courses add +0.5. An A in an AP class = 5.0, an A in a regular class = 4.0.

Which GPA do colleges look at?

Most colleges look at both. Weighted GPA shows course rigor — how hard you challenged yourself. Unweighted GPA gives a standardized baseline across all schools.
