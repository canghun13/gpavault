What's included in cost of attendance?

Cost of attendance (COA) includes tuition, fees, room and board, books, transportation, and personal expenses. This is the number colleges use to calculate financial aid eligibility.

Average costs in 2026

Public in-state: ~$27,000/year. Public out-of-state: ~$44,000/year. Private nonprofit: ~$57,000/year. These are sticker prices before aid.
