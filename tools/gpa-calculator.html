How GPA is calculated

GPA (Grade Point Average) is calculated by multiplying each course's grade points by its credit hours, summing those totals, then dividing by total credit hours.

Formula: GPA = Σ (Grade Points × Credit Hours) ÷ Total Credit Hours

Grade point scale

Most US colleges use a 4.0 scale: A = 4.0, A− = 3.7, B+ = 3.3, B = 3.0, B− = 2.7, C+ = 2.3, C = 2.0, C− = 1.7, D+ = 1.3, D = 1.0, F = 0.0.

Semester vs. cumulative GPA

This calculator computes the GPA for the courses you enter. For cumulative GPA across multiple semesters, enter all your courses together.
