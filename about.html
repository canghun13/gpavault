About GPA Vault

GPA Vault is a free calculator hub built for students navigating one of the most financially complex decisions of their lives — college.

From calculating your GPA to figuring out if your degree is worth the debt, we cover every number you'll need from application to graduation.

Why we built this

Most financial tools online are built for adults managing mortgages and retirement accounts. Students have completely different needs — GPA thresholds, loan limits, SAT score conversions, semester-by-semester grade tracking.

GPA Vault focuses entirely on that slice: practical, accurate, and free.

Our approach

Every calculator uses formulas based on how schools, the College Board, and the Department of Education actually calculate these numbers. No guesswork, no hidden assumptions.
